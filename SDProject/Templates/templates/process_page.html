Step-by-Step Process

1. Start by clicking on the "Create Set" button.

2. Enter a relevant name for your set.

3. Save the set.

4. Enter the number of questions for the set, along with their respective answers and optional hints, then save.

5. Use the "Find Set" button to locate your saved set.

6. Click on the desired set to open it.

7. This will take you to the flashcard view, where cards are shown one by one.

8. If you saved any hints, click the "Hint" button to view them.

9. Navigate through the questions using the "Next" or "Previous" buttons.

10. To return to the home page, click the "Back to Home" button.
