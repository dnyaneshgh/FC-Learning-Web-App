LEARN THE EASY WAY!

CREATE | SAVE | REVISE

Step-by-Step Process

1. Start by clicking on "Create Flashcard" tab

2. Enter the details for the flashcard, then save it. Adding a hint is optional

3. Click on "Find Flashcard" to locate your saved flashcard

4. Click on the desired flashcard to open it

5. This will take you to the flashcard view, where cards are shown one by one

6.If you saved any hint, click the "Hint" button to view it

7. Navigate through the questions using the "<" or ">" buttons.

8. To return to the home page, click the "Home" tab
